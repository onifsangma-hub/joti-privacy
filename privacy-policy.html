1. Who This App Is For

Joti AI Translator is a real-time voice translation app for adults (18 years and older) working in professional settings including nail salons, restaurants, hotels, retail stores, taxi services, and medical reception desks.

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child used this App, please contact us using the details in Section 13.

2. What This App Does

Joti AI Translator listens to spoken words through your device microphone, converts speech to text using your device's built-in speech recognition, sends that text to the Google Cloud Translation API to produce a translated text, and plays the translation aloud through your device speaker or connected earphones.

The App supports English, Spanish, and Bengali and can translate automatically between all three languages.

4. Data Shared With Third Parties

One type of data. One third party. One purpose. Transcribed text is sent to Google Cloud Translation API to generate translations. Nothing else is shared with anyone.

Google Cloud Translation API

When you speak a phrase, your device's speech recognition converts your voice to text. That text is then sent to Google's Cloud Translation API to produce the translated text.

PropertyDetail

No other third party receives any data from this App. There is no advertising network, analytics provider, social media SDK, data broker, or crash-reporting service connected to this App.

For Google's privacy practices regarding Cloud Translation: cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice

5. Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device microphone to capture your speech for translation. Microphone access is used only while the App is open and one of the following is true:

  • You have tapped the microphone button, or
  • Auto Work Mode or Real Salon Work Mode is enabled and Break Mode is off.

How audio is processed: Your device's operating system (Android or iOS) handles microphone audio internally. Audio is converted to text by your device's built-in speech recognition engine. The App receives only the converted text — not audio samples, audio files, or any audio data. The App never records, saves, or transmits audio.

Break Mode: Enabling Break Mode immediately stops all microphone activity, cancels any active speech recognition session, and halts TTS playback. While Break Mode is on, no speech is captured or processed.

Revoking permission: You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device's Settings → Apps → Joti AI Translator → Permissions → Microphone. The App will display a guidance message and will not access the microphone while permission is denied.

Note on Android speech recognition: Android's default speech recognizer may send audio to Google's servers as part of the operating system's speech recognition service. This behavior is controlled by the Android OS and your device settings — not by this App. You can enable on-device-only speech recognition in your Android settings if your device supports it.

6. Speaker Mode and Earphone Mode

Speaker Mode and Earphone Mode are in-app settings that control audio playback. They adjust pitch, speed, and audio routing to your phone speaker, wired earphones, or Bluetooth earbuds (AirPods, Galaxy Buds, or other Bluetooth headsets).

These settings:

  • Do not transmit any data to any server
  • Do not connect to any external service
  • Are stored on your device as local preferences
  • Use your device's standard audio system for Bluetooth routing

8. Analytics and Advertising

Analytics: This App collects no analytics data whatsoever. No usage events, screen views, session durations, button taps, or performance metrics are sent anywhere. There is no analytics SDK installed in the App.

Advertising: This App displays no advertisements. There is no advertising SDK installed. No advertising identifier is accessed or shared.

Crash reporting: This App does not use crash reporting software. No crash logs, error traces, or diagnostic data are sent to any server. Errors are shown only as local status messages within the App.

9. Data Security

Data stored on your device is protected by Android's application sandboxing. No other app on your device can access the data stored by Joti AI Translator.

All data sent to Google Cloud Translation is transmitted over HTTPS using standard TLS encryption. No unencrypted connections are used.

The App does not implement user authentication because no user account is required. There is no password, login, or personal profile.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Delete all stored data

Open the App → scroll to the Privacy section → tap Clear History. This immediately and permanently removes all conversation phrases, translations, timestamps, favorites, categories, and learning counters stored on your device. This action cannot be undone.

Revoke microphone access

Go to your device Settings → Apps → Joti AI Translator → Permissions → Microphone → select Deny. The App will stop capturing speech and display a guidance message.

Uninstall the App

Uninstalling the App from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

Translation data

We do not retain your translation text after the translation response is received. We have no mechanism to retrieve specific translation requests made to Google Cloud because we do not log them and they are processed transiently. For requests related to Google's processing of your data, visit Google's privacy controls at myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.
